US Department of
Health and Human Services (HHS)

This site is primarily a landing page to direct visitors to HHS-related GitHub accounts, repositories and pages that are maintained under various names by HHS operating divisions, and staff offices. HHS, as an umbrella organization, has its own GitHub organizational account that hosts repos, and a GitHub.io page.

HHS protects health and provides essential human services. HHS has 11 operating divisions, including 8 agencies in the US Public Health Service and 3 human services agencies that administer a wide variety of health and human services and conduct life-saving research for the nation. HHS has 17 staff offices that oversee operations and provide guidance on policy issues and program implementation.
